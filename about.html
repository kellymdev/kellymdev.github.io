About

Kelly Munro is a New Zealand based nature, wildlife and landscape photographer. She is a member of the Photographic Society of New Zealand (PSNZ) and the Kapiti Camera Club. Her work has featured in exhibitions in both New Zealand and Australia.

Exhibitions

  • 2015 - NATEX - Photographic Society of New Zealand National Exhibition - Tauranga
  • 2013 - 17 Projects Group Show - Photospace Gallery, Wellington
  • 2010 - Canon Photo5 Exhibition - Carriageworks, Sydney, Australia
